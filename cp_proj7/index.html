Vertigo Shot

Introduction

In this project, we are going to duplicate an in-camera effect: Vertigo shot. The idea is simple, we choose a object, and keep zooming in the camera as we step away from the object to maintain the size of the object. First object we chose is a mailbox in front of the Mulford Hall, and the second one is a fire hydrant in front of the Engineering Library. The camera we used is a Sony Nex-7.
